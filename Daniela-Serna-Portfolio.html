About Me

I have a background in arts administration and currently work at two arts nonprofits: Open Signal (formerly Portland Community Media) as engagement coordinator and S1 as curator and facilitator. My focus is creating avenues of access for all to engage with creativity, particularly historically marginalized and underrepresented populations.

I am currently a student through the Epicodus Intro to Programming course. I chose to take this course to bolster my skills and find a job that will merge my programming skills and my arts administration background.

In my free time I also work as DJ, and am currently a resident at Club Culo and Club Tropicana. My sound can be described as diasporic club, bringing together the sounds of the global underground. My mixes can be found on my Soundcloud.
